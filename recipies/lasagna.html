Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is so yummy and delicious,
you will enjoy eating it thoroughly

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs

Instructions

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest
